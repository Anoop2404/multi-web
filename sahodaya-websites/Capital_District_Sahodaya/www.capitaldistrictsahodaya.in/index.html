Tarang 2025

The idea of Sahodaya Schools complexes was first mooted by the Education Commission,1966. It was the National Policy on Education, 1966 which reiterated in strong words the need for promoting school complexes for meeting the quality of education.

The first national conference of Principals of CBSE affiliated schools organized by CBSE in collaboration with APSC Madras in 1985 emphasized collaboration among schools and establishing linkages in academic and other co-curricular and cultural activities.

About Tarang

The year 1987 saw a further boost to the idea of setting of SS complex (es) when CBSE in pursuance of the National Policy of Education, 1986, used a booklet titled "Implementation of National policy on Education in CBSE affiliated Schools-Programme of Action for three years" which reiterated school complexes and resolved to encourage the process of setting of Sahodaya School Complexes.
