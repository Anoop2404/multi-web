ABOUT US

Thrissur Central Sahodaya

Thrissur Central Sahodaya is a collective of CBSE-Affiliated schools in Central Thrissur, serving as a collaborative platform for member schools to share and contribute ideas, challenges, solutions, and resources. The concept of Sahodaya, translating to “Rising Together,” was established in 1986 with the aim of fostering collaborative ideas among CBSE schools to achieve excellence in education.
