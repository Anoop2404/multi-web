About us

Central Travancore Sahodaya Complex

Sahodaya School Complexes [SSC] which flagged off in the year 1986, is a voluntary association of schools in a given area, who through mutual choices, have agreed to come together for a systematic and system wide renewal of the total educational process. The significance of Sahodaya is to rise together and to share their innovative practices in the field of academic procedures, pedagogy, evaluation, value oriented learning and professional growth for teachers. Through positive promotional efforts, the Board has helped schools come together and form an interactive and sharing relationship. It is indeed an interactive platform for schools to deliberate upon the different policies and guidelines of the Board and provide effective feedback on their implementation to establish new benchmarks of quality. Central Travancore Sahodaya Complex established in 1995, is an association of CBSE schools in Central Kerala formulated to organize various programmes useful for teachers & students. CTSC is organizing Kalotsav for its member schools mainly to identify and promote the inherent talents of the students and to channelize them for the reformation of a virtuous society. The motto of Sahodaya is 'Caring and Sharing'.
