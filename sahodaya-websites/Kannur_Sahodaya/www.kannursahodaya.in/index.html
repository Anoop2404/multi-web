About Us

Sahodaya School Complex Kannur

Kannur Sahodaya School Complex is an Association of CBSE Schools, registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Reg. No. KNR/CA/304/2023). It unites CBSE-affiliated schools across Kannur district, Kerala, to foster collaboration, academic excellence, and holistic student development.
Inspired by the CBSE’s Sahodaya philosophy—meaning rising together—the complex organizes inter-school events such as talent search examinations, cultural festivals, sports meets, and teacher development programs. By promoting shared learning and innovation, Kannur Sahodaya empowers schools to grow collectively and deliver quality education.
