Hearty Welcome…

Alappuzha Sahodaya is the official organ of CBSE Schools in Central Kerala. Presently there are 42 schools associated with Alappuzha Sahodaya . CBSE insists that all schools take membership in a Sahodaya.

There are many activities like Training Workshops for Senior Secondary and Secondary and KG Teachers, School Quality Assessment & Accreditation Workshops, Student Leadership Camps, Inter- School Kalotsav, Inter School Sports Meet, Inter School Games- such as Football, Basket Ball and Volley Ball . Besides we set Common Timetable- SA I & SA II .
